Steps to Setup Wishlist

  1. Go to Amazon.com.

  2. Go to "Accounts & Lists"

  3. Go to "Your Lists"

  4. Create a list

  5. Name your list and save it

  6. This is the fun part! Add items to your list that you need for your classroom.

  7. Go to your list and hover over the "More" button, then click "Manage List"

  8. In "Manage List," set privacy to "Shared" and your preferred shipping address. Do not share your shipping address.

  9. Click "Send list to others"

  10. Click "View only" and finally hit "Copy link"
